East Africa's $250B Opportunity

Four sectors driving the region's transformation — and your entry points into each.

🌾 Agribusiness — 60% Uncultivated Land, $12B Export Potential

East Africa's agricultural sector remains largely untapped. Kenya avocado exports have tripled in 3 years. Ethiopian coffee commands premium global prices. With 60% of arable land uncultivated and a growing export infrastructure, agribusiness presents one of the highest-ROI opportunities on the continent.

💻 Silicon Savannah — Nairobi as Africa's Tech Capital

Nairobi has attracted $1.2B in venture capital in 2025 alone. M-Pesa pioneered mobile money globally. The Konza Technopolis smart city is operational. East Africa's tech ecosystem spans fintech, agritech, healthtech and e-commerce — with deep talent pools and improving connectivity across all 5 nations.

🦒 Tourism Boom — Serengeti, Kilimanjaro & $5.2B Revenue

East Africa's tourism industry generates $5.2B annually with 12% year-on-year growth. The Serengeti, Maasai Mara, Kilimanjaro, and Zanzibar beaches attract high-value visitors. Opportunities span luxury lodges, adventure tourism, cultural experiences, and tourism technology platforms.

🏭 Manufacturing Hub — Duty-Free EPZs & COMESA Access

Ethiopia's 16 industrial parks and Kenya's Export Processing Zones offer duty-free manufacturing with COMESA market access. Global brands have established manufacturing here. Low labour costs, improving infrastructure, and trade agreements make this a compelling nearshoring alternative to South and Southeast Asia.
